Washington law sets notice requirements and limits for landlord entry, with narrow exceptions for emergencies. [wa-rcw-5918150:RCW 59.18.150]

Outside emergencies, entries should occur with proper advance notice and at reasonable times. [wa-rcw-5918150:RCW 59.18.150]

Document each entry event with date, time, reason given, and whether notice was provided. [editorial]

Send written communication requesting compliance with notice rules and propose reasonable access windows for repairs or inspections. [editorial]

If unauthorized entries continue, contact legal aid or a tenant-rights organization to review available remedies. [editorial]
