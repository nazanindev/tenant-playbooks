After move-out, Washington law requires landlords to return the security deposit or provide a written, itemized statement of deductions within the required timeframe. [wa-rcw-5918280:RCW 59.18.280]

Seattle guidance explains deposit return expectations and common dispute points for renters. [seattle-deposit-returns]

Send your forwarding address in writing and keep proof of delivery and all landlord communications. [editorial]

Preserve move-out evidence (photos, videos, and condition notes) in case deductions are disputed. [editorial]

If deadlines are missed or deductions appear improper, contact legal aid to evaluate recovery options. [wa-rcw-5918280:RCW 59.18.280]
