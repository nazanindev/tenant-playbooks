If you cannot pay rent, contact your landlord in writing as early as possible and ask for a payment plan. Early communication can help avoid escalation. [editorial]

Washington law requires a specific notice process for nonpayment before a landlord can proceed toward eviction. [wa-rcw-5918057:RCW 59.18.057]

Eviction in Seattle still requires a court process; a notice is not the same as an immediate lockout. [seattle-unlawful-detainer-guide]

Keep records of all payments, messages, and assistance applications so you can document your efforts and payment history. [editorial]

If you receive court papers, respond quickly and seek legal aid immediately. [seattle-unlawful-detainer-guide]
