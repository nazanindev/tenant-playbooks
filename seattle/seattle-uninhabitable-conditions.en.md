Washington law requires landlords to maintain rental units in a fit and habitable condition and to make necessary repairs. [wa-rcw-5918060:RCW 59.18.060]

Document every serious condition with date-stamped photos, videos, and a written log of hazards and impacts. [editorial]

Notify your landlord in writing about each issue and request repairs. Keep copies of your notice and all responses. [editorial]

After written notice, Washington law provides remedies if required repairs are not made within the applicable timelines. [wa-rcw-5918090:RCW 59.18.090]

If dangerous conditions persist, contact local inspection services and legal aid to assess enforcement options and next steps. [editorial]
